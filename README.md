# Guideline Guru - AI Creative Compliance Assistant

**Guideline Guru** is a simple AI-inspired prototype that helps advertisers and small business owners check if their ad creatives follow brand guidelines like logo placement, color, font, and text area ratio.

Built for the **Tesco Retail Media InnovAItion Jam 2025**, this tool focuses on making ad review faster, smarter, and more consistent.

---

## Idea

In marketing, brands spend hours manually checking whether their creatives meet their design rules.  
Guideline Guru makes this process quick and simple — just upload your ad creative and get instant feedback with a compliance score.

---

## Tech Stack

- **HTML5**
- **Tailwind CSS**
- **JavaScript (Vanilla JS)**  
*(Frontend-only prototype — no backend used)*

---

## Features

  Upload ad creative image  
  Instant AI-simulated feedback  
  Randomized compliance score  
  Simple and responsive Tailwind UI  
  Before-upload info section + After-upload result section  

---

## How to Use

1. Clone or download this repository  
2. Open the folder  
3. Double-click on **index.html** to open it in your browser  
4. Upload an image and click **Analyze**  
5. See your feedback and compliance score appear on the right
